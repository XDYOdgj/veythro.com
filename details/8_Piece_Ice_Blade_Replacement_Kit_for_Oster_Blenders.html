Product Highlights

  • Advanced Ice Crushing Technology: Featuring a stainless steel blade with reversing blade technology, this set ensures smooth and efficient blending by drawing food and ice down during operation.
  • Durable Rubber Gaskets: High-quality sealing components to maintain blade alignment and prevent leakage, ensuring a longer-lasting performance.
  • Universal Fit: Designed to work with most Oster and Osterizer blenders, including 5 & 6 Cup Jars and Counter-Top Models, for hassle-free replacement and maintenance.
  • Complete Replacement Kit: Includes 1x Crusher 4 Blade, 1x Crusher Pro 6 Blade, 1x Bottom Base Cap, 1x Coupling Stud Slinger Pin, and 4x Sealing O-Ring Gaskets for all-in-one repair solutions.
  • Not Original: These parts are not original Oster products, but high-quality alternatives designed to meet the same performance standards.

Why Choose This Kit?

Whether you're a home cook or a food enthusiast, maintaining your blender's performance is essential. This 8-piece replacement parts kit is crafted with precision and care to ensure your blending experience remains smooth, efficient, and reliable. Ideal for users who want to keep their appliances running like new without the hassle of ordering original parts.

Description

The MEET JUICE 8-Piece Replacement Parts Kit includes essential components for your Oster and Osterizer blender, such as blades, rubber gaskets, and a coupling stud slinger pin. Made for consistent blending, these parts are compatible with most models, including 5 & 6 cup glass and plastic jars, and counter-top units. The reversing blade technology ensures that food and ice are drawn down effectively, leading to a smoother and more uniform result. This kit is perfect for those looking to extend the life of their blender with reliable, cost-effective replacement parts. Keep in mind that these parts are not original Oster products and are manufactured as alternatives with the same quality and function.
