6-Fin Ninja Blender Blade Replacement

Perfectly designed for easy replacement and seamless compatibility with your Ninja Auto iQ blender. This blade is ideal for everyday cooking and blending tasks.

Important Compatibility Check

Before purchasing, please double-check that your original blade has 6 fins and a female socket. The blade has a diameter of about 4 inches (10cm) and is compatible with Nutri Ninja models featuring a 10cm cup opening, including BL480, BL480D, BL482, BL456, and others. It is not compatible with models like BL660, BL663, BL770, or BL771 that use 7-fin blades or have an 8.5cm cup opening.

Durable & Safe Material

Constructed with BPA-free plastic and high-quality stainless steel, this blade is built to last. It is suitable for regular kitchen use and can be safely placed in the dishwasher for easy cleaning.

Ideal for Daily Use

Great for making smoothies, shakes, and other soft ingredients. This blade is suited for simple, routine blending tasks in your home kitchen, ensuring efficiency and performance each time.

Package Includes

Every package contains 1x 6-Fin blade assembly. If you're replacing the gasket, remember to remove the existing one from the blade base to prevent double layering and potential leaks. This helps in maintaining optimal functionality and hygienic blending.
