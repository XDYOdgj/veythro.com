Vitamix Personal Cup Adapter - 61724, Clear

Take your Vitamix blending experience on the go with the Personal Cup Adapter. Designed for use with legacy-series Vitamix machines, this versatile accessory lets you make individual smoothies, shakes, or quick chopped ingredients for any recipe. Whether you're on the move or need to prepare single-serving blends at home, this adapter is a game-changer.

The dual-purpose 20-ounce blending containers transform into spill-proof travel cups, making it easy to blend on the go without messy spills. The storage lid is made of durable polypropylene, ensuring a secure and leak-free experience. The blade base is constructed from delrene, the same high-quality material used in your full-size Vitamix machine for strength and longevity.

Both the lid and blade base are dishwasher-safe, so cleaning up is simple and hassle-free. Ideal for busy lifestyles and daily use, this adapter offers convenience without compromising on performance or safety.

Compatible with Vitamix Classic machines including 5200, 5300, 6300, 6500, 7500, 780, Aspire Series, Creations Series, Professional Series, Total Nutrition Center, TurboBlend Series, and Explorian Series. Please note: it is not compatible with the Vitamix Ascent Series. Elevate your blending routine with this essential upgrade.
