GahsElec Blender Cups for NutriBullet Replacement Parts

Upgrade your NutriBullet experience with our premium replacement cups. Designed for compatibility with NutriBullet 600W and 900W models, these 32oz cups come in a 2-pack and are perfect for those looking to maintain the performance and functionality of their personal blender.

Crafted from high-quality food-grade plastic and silicone, these cups offer clear visibility and are both dishwasher safe and easy to clean. The included flip-top to-go-lid ensures convenience, while the rubber gaskets provide a secure seal and prevent leaks.

What you'll receive: Two 32oz cups, two flip-top to-go-lids, and two durable rubber gaskets. Each piece is designed to perfectly match your original NutriBullet 600W or NutriBullet Pro 900W blender parts.

Before purchasing, we recommend verifying compatibility with your specific blender model. These cups are not intended for use with NutriBullet Pro 1000, NutriBullet Pro+ 1200, NutriBullet Ultra, NutriBullet Lean, NutriBullet RX, Magic Bullet, or Nutri Ninja blenders.

By ensuring proper compatibility, you can enjoy a seamless replacement experience and maintain the efficiency of your kitchen appliance. Shop with confidence and choose the right fit for your device.
