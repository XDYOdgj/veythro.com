Durable Replacement Outer Bowl Lid for Ninja Ice Cream Maker

Elevate your ice cream making experience with a reliable and high-quality replacement lid. Made from premium clear polycarbonate, this lid is built to last while maintaining the same design and functionality as the original. Unlike cheaper alternatives, our lid is dishwasher safe, making cleanup effortless and saving you time and hassle.

Perfect Fit for Specific Models

This versatile lid is compatible with a range of Ninja ice cream maker series including NC299AMZ, NC300, NC300C, NC301, CN301CO, CN305A, NC301HNV, NC301HRD, NC301HRG, NC301HSL, NC301HWH, and NC301RGBBB. It's ideal for those who need a dependable replacement to keep their appliance in top condition. Please note that this lid is not compatible with the Ninja Deluxe (NC500 series) ice cream makers.

A Cost-Effective Upgrade

Need a new cover for your ice cream machine bowl? Our product offers an affordable solution to repair or replace a damaged, worn, or moldy lid. It allows you to get your machine back in action quickly without spending a fortune. Whether you're a home cook or a hobbyist, this replacement lid will enhance your ice cream making process with excellent value.

Important Note

Please be aware that this product includes only the outer bowl cover. The replacement paddle blade is not included - make sure to check your existing parts or order separately if needed.
