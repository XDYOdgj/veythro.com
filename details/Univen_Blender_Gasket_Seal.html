Univen Blender Gasket Seal

This high-quality gasket seal is designed specifically for Black and Decker blenders. It ensures a secure fit between the blade and the jar bottom, providing reliable performance and preventing leaks during use. The seal measures 2.15" (54.6mm) inner diameter by 2.83" (72mm) outer diameter, making it compatible with a range of models.

The Univen Blender Gasket Seal is a must-have replacement part for homeowners who rely on their blenders for smoothies, shakes, and other food preparations. Its precision engineering guarantees a perfect fit and long-lasting durability, helping extend the life of your blender. Whether you're a professional chef or a casual home cook, this gasket seal offers the reliability you need for consistent blending results.

Ideal for Black & Decker blender models including but not limited to BLC10650H, BLC10650HB, BLC10650MB, BLC12600B, BLC12650H, BLC12650HB, BLC12650HM, BLC12750HM, BLC12750HMS, BLC18750DB, and BLC18750DBC, this gasket seal ensures optimal sealing and a smooth operation. Replace worn or damaged seals with confidence and enjoy trouble-free kitchen use every time.
