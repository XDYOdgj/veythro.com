Heavy Duty Pan Seal Kit for Breadman Bread Maker Model TR2500BC

Upgrade your bread maker with this durable and reliable Pan Seal Kit Replacement. Specifically designed for the 3-Rivet Pan Version of the Breadman Automatic Dough & Bread Maker Machine Model TR2500BC, this kit ensures a secure seal to prevent leaks during baking.

What's Included

  • Complete set of Pan Seal Replacement Parts
  • Compatible only with Breadman TR2500BC 3-Rivet Pan Version
  • Includes Snap Ring Pliers for easy installation
  • May require an E-clip installation tool for proper setup
  • Ensures your bread maker functions like new again

Why You Need It

If you've noticed water pooling beneath the pan while baking, it's likely due to a damaged seal. This Heavy Duty Pan Seal Kit is the perfect solution to restore your machine's performance and extend its lifespan. It is not suitable for pans without a visible red seal from the top side, so make sure your pan is compatible before purchasing.

Easy Installation & User-Friendly

Designed for simple use, this Pan Seal Kit Replacement allows you to easily maintain your bread maker. A little water under the pan is a common issue that can be quickly resolved with this kit. By replacing the seal, you help ensure that your ingredients stay dry and your bread is baked to perfection every time.

Improve Your Baking Experience

Enjoy flawless baking with a properly sealed pan. This kit is a must-have for any Breadman TR2500BC user who wants to keep their machine in top condition. It offers a practical, cost-effective solution to common maintenance issues, making it the best choice for those who value reliability and efficiency in their kitchen appliances.

How to Replace

For detailed instructions on how to replace your pan seal, search for a video online. This will guide you through the process and make installation a breeze. Make sure to remove the old seal entirely before installing the new one to ensure a proper fit and function.
