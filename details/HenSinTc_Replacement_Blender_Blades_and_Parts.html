Replacement Parts for Hamilton Beach Blender Blades

Upgrade your blender with high-quality replacement components that include a blade, a base bottom cap, and two rubber sealing gaskets. These parts are designed to fit most Hamilton Beach blenders, making it easy to maintain your kitchen appliance without replacing the whole unit.

What's Included

  • 1 replacement blade
  • 1 base bottom cap
  • 2 rubber sealing gaskets

Dimensions

- Cap size: 2.68 inch x 1.05 inch
- Rubber O-ring outer size: 2.6 inch x 2 inch
- Inner O-ring size: 2 inch
- Blade size: OD 67.5mm / 2.66 inch

Why Choose This Set

Crafted from durable stainless steel, our blade ensures efficient blending and the ability to crush ice with ease. The rubber gaskets offer a secure, leak-proof seal, while the base cap is made of hard plastic for long-lasting use. These parts are ideal for those who want to extend the life of their Hamilton Beach blender without investing in a new one.

Compatibility

This set is compatible with a wide range of Hamilton Beach blender models, including 50122, 5817, 54200, 52254, 53516C, 542100, 50200, 50199, 640-4, 54250, 53521, 600-2, 54100, 50200MP, 582-4, 53603, #56200, 53600, 52401. Please check the 2nd image to ensure compatibility with your specific model.

Note

This product does not fit the Wavestation or Waveaction models, nor some other specific models like 50256, 54200R, 58200, 50235, 52171, 50220, E52210, 50104, 54221, 58149, 50120. If you're unsure about compatibility, compare the sizes with your blender to find the perfect fit.
