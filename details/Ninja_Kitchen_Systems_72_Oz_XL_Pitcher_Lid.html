Ninja Kitchen Systems 72 oz XL Pitcher Locking Lid - Auto IQ Only

This 72 oz XL Pitcher Locking Lid is the perfect accessory for your Ninja Auto IQ blender. Designed for convenience and durability, it ensures a secure fit and easy pouring experience. Ideal for everyday use, this lid is made to last and is built for users who value efficiency in the kitchen.

Key Features

  • Comes with an extended pour spout for easy liquid dispensing.
  • Secure locking mechanism to keep your ingredients safe and contained.
  • Durable construction for long-lasting performance.
  • Compatibility with selected Ninja Auto IQ and Duo models only.

What It Fits

This lid is specifically designed for Ninja Auto IQ and Duo models. Check the product images for confirmation of compatibility with your specific unit.

Not Compatible With

Please note that this lid does not fit Ninja Mega, Supra, Ultra, Pro, or other large pitchers like the BL771, BL770, BL780, NJ600, or BL700 Square Top Pitchers. It is strictly for Auto IQ and Duo models with extended pour spouts only.

Why You'll Love It

Upgrade your blending experience with a lid that combines style and functionality. Whether you're making fresh smoothies, shakes, or other beverages, this accessory ensures a clean, spill-proof, and easy-to-use solution that saves you time and effort in the kitchen.
