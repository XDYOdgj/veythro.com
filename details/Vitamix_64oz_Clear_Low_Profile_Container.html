Vitamix Container, 64oz. Low-Profile, Clear

Effortlessly prepare large batches with the Vitamix 64oz. Low-Profile Container. Its lightweight and compact design makes it easy to store under most kitchen cabinets, ideal for small spaces without sacrificing performance. Made from durable and safe BPA-free Eastman Tritan Copolyester, this container is built to last while keeping your ingredients fresh and visible.

Compatible with Vitamix G-Series Machines and the Vitamix 5300, this container offers reliable performance for everyday blending needs. It features a 4-inch diameter cutting blade that ensures thorough mixing and consistency. Note that it is not designed for use with Vitamix Ascent Series machines.

The high-speed blades within the container create enough friction heat to warm cold ingredients in just six minutes, making it perfect for quick heating and blending applications. Its clear design allows you to monitor your ingredients as they blend, adding convenience and control to your kitchen routine.

Precise measurements are included on the container and even on the lid plug, with a 2-ounce line for easy portion control. The soft-grip handle provides comfort and stability whether you're pouring or using the tamper, ensuring a smooth and efficient blending experience.

Upgrade your blending setup with a container that combines functionality, durability, and style. Whether you're making smoothies, soups, or sauces, this Vitamix container delivers professional results at home.
