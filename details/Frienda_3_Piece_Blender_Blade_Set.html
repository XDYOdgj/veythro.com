Frienda 3-Piece Blender Blade Assembly Set

Enhance your blender performance with the Frienda 3-piece blade assembly set, designed for easy replacement and installation. This set includes one versatile blade assembly and one drive socket kit, which consists of a socket and wrench, making it the perfect combination for all your maintenance needs.

Comprehensive Compatibility

Ideal for Vita-Mix blenders, including models 1151, 1152, and many others like 1002, 1230, 5006, 5012, 5057, BarBoss 5039, VITA-MIX, VM0100, VM0100A, Total Nutrition Centers, Vita-Prep, and Drink Machine models. It also works with standard household and commercial containers, such as 64 oz, 32 oz, and 48 oz sizes.

Smooth Replacement Process

The installation process is simple and user-friendly. Just remove the rubber pad, extract the old socket kit, and install the new one using the wrench provided. With only three straightforward steps, you can complete the replacement in just a few minutes.

Optimize Blender Performance

If you've noticed your blender blades are turning slower than they used to, but your motor is still in good condition, it's a clear sign that your socket drive may need replacement. It's recommended to inspect your drive socket every 3 to 4 months to ensure optimal performance and longevity.

Whether you're a home chef or a professional in the kitchen, this Frienda blade assembly set offers reliable, durable parts that keep your blender working efficiently and effectively.
