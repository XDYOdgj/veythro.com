Our blades are crafted to fit perfectly and deliver optimal performance, ensuring that your kitchen appliances function smoothly and efficiently.

What's Included
  • 2 High-Speed Blender Blades
  • 2 Extra Spare Rubber Gaskets

Built with food-grade 304 stainless steel and BPA-free materials, our blades are durable, easy to clean, and safe for everyday use.
